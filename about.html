About Bangin Baggerz

Founded by audio expert Rob, Bangin Baggerz was born from a passion for Harley-Davidson baggers and a love of powerful sound. With over 20 years in audio installation, we bring concert-quality audio to your bike with precision and style.

We believe your ride should sound as bold as it looks. Our mission: help fellow riders experience music on the road like never before.
